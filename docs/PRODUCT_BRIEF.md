# Product Brief

## One-Line Pitch

LifeNuance AI routes everyday life questions to specialized AI assistants with explicit domain boundaries and structured next steps.

## Problem

People increasingly ask general AI tools for complicated life decisions: education planning, legal questions, property projects, local activities, care decisions, and family logistics. General chatbots often mix domains, overstate confidence, or fail to explain when professional help is needed.

## Initial Customer

Busy individuals and families who want practical planning support without becoming experts in every domain.

## MVP

1. Register assistant modules with scope, boundaries, keywords, and intake questions.
2. Route a user request to the most relevant assistant.
3. Return a domain-aware plan, caveats, and suggested follow-up questions.
4. Keep high-risk domains clearly bounded.

## Differentiation

LifeNuance is not a single persona chatbot. It is a registry of assistants with domain-specific intake, boundaries, and evaluation.

## Future Modules

- Education planning
- Legal information navigation
- Property and remodel planning
- Local activities
- Family care coordination
- Finance planning guidance
- Health appointment preparation
